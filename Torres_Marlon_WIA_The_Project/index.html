DJ MGG Interview

By Lauren McEwen
Real Atlanta Magazine

Amateur disc jockeys can mix together enough hits and filler songs to avoid having the crowd throw drinks at them while chanting, "Kill the DJ" in disgust. A great DJ, however, can do much more. They can connect with the crowd, playing enough bangers to illicit the "that's-my-song syndrome," complete with the dropped jaw, wide eyes, and instant dancing.

DJ MGG prides himself on being able to give his audience what they want. "I work with a little of everything. Old school hip hop—old school anything. Even old school Latin music," says DJ MGG. "Most DJs play music by sets. I read crowds. I vibe with what the crowd wants."
